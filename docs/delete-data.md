# Delete your data — Days in Office

This page explains how to remove all data the **Days in Office** Android app
(developer: Rodrigo Carvalho) has stored on your device. Days in Office is a
single-user productivity app — all data stays on the device that runs the app,
and there is no server, account, or cloud copy to delete separately.

## Steps to delete all in-app data

1. Open the **Days in Office** app on your device.
2. Tap **Settings** (gear icon in the top bar).
3. Tap **Privacy**.
4. Tap **Delete all data**.
5. Confirm the dialog.

After confirmation, the app's local database and saved preferences are wiped
immediately. You can also uninstall the app to remove everything — see the
"Uninstalling" note below.

## What is deleted

The *Delete all data* action removes, from your device:

* All recorded office days (date, detection method, notes).
* All PTO and holiday entries you've marked.
* All saved geofences and Wi-Fi entries (office location settings).
* Your mandate target (percentage, period, working days).
* Detection preferences (which detection methods are enabled).
* Notification history and any pending detection prompts.

## What is kept

Nothing about you is kept by the app or by the developer. The app has no
server-side component, so there is no off-device copy of any data.

Android system-level data not owned by the app is **not** affected:

* System location-permission grants remain assigned to the app until you
  revoke them in Android Settings.
* Android's standard backup mechanism may copy app data to your Google account
  unless you have disabled "Back up to Google Drive" for this app. To be
  thorough, disable backup for the app before uninstalling.

## Uninstalling

Uninstalling Days in Office from your device removes the app and its local
data store. Subject to the Android backup behaviour above, this leaves no
trace of the app's data on the device.
